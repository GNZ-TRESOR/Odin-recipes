rainbow cake

rain bow cake it is so called this due to the color that makes up the cake and it can be enjoyed by adults and kids also it is good for parties try it tests refreshing...

    ingredient
  • 360 butter softened
  • 360 custer sugar
  • 360 self-raising flour
  • 6 large eggs
  • food coloring gel

    steps to making rainbow cake
  1. heat oven 180
  2. greace three 20cm cake tins
  3. 180g of flour,custer,butter, 3 eggs,2 tps of vanilla,20ml of milk
  4. mix the mixer till its creamy
  5. add coloring gel
  6. put in the oven 12-15 min
  7. allow the cooling
